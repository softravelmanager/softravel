
# Italian Airplane Ticketing Agency - Single Page App

## Overview
A beautiful, fully functional single-page website for an Italian airplane ticketing agency featuring a modern Mediterranean design with warm Italian-inspired colors (azure blue, terracotta accents, clean whites).

---

## Sections to Build

### 1. **Hero Section with Flight Search Form**
- Eye-catching hero with an Italian travel background
- Flight search form with:
  - Departure city
  - Destination city
  - Departure & Return dates
  - Number of passengers
  - Class selection (Economy/Business/First)
- "Search Flights" button that sends inquiry via email

### 2. **Most Loved Destinations**
- Grid of 6-8 popular destinations (mix of Italian and European cities)
- Beautiful destination cards with images, city names, and starting prices
- Destinations like Rome, Milan, Venice, Paris, Barcelona, London, Amsterdam

### 3. **Why Choose Us Section**
- 4 compelling reasons with icons:
  - Best Price Guarantee
  - 24/7 Customer Support
  - Secure Booking
  - Italian Expertise / Local Knowledge

### 4. **Stats/Numbers Section**
- Impressive counters displaying:
  - Happy Customers (e.g., 50,000+)
  - Destinations Covered
  - Years of Experience
  - Partner Airlines

### 5. **Customer Reviews Section**
- Carousel or grid of 4-6 customer testimonials
- Star ratings, customer names, and review text
- Professional, trustworthy appearance

### 6. **Contact Us Section**
- Contact form with Name, Email, Phone, Message
- Form submits inquiry via email
- Company contact information displayed

### 7. **Footer**
- Company logo and tagline
- Quick navigation links
- Contact information (address, phone, email)
- Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter)
- Copyright notice

---

## Backend Setup (Required for Email)
- **Resend** email service for sending form submissions
- Edge function to handle form submissions securely

---

## Design Details
- **Color Palette**: Azure blue, white, terracotta accents, navy for text
- **Typography**: Clean, modern fonts
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly design
- **Animations**: Subtle scroll animations for engagement
